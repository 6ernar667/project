About Us

Almaty Hiking is a mountain route viewer that makes hiking in the mountains easier and safer. Our clients are everyone who wants to visit the mountains without having to worry about their safety.

Service purpose

The goal of this product is to create a service that allows users to view routes to specific locations in the mountains. The main feature of this service is that people can see routes to different places in the mountains. Routes will have safety statuses: green(safe), yellow(medium), red(dangerous)
